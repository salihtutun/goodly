## Persona 1: Maria — The Overwhelmed Restaurant Owner

### Demographics
- **Age:** 42
- **Business:** "Maria's Italian Kitchen" — family restaurant, 5 years old
- **Location:** Portland, OR
- **Employees:** 8
- **Monthly revenue:** $45,000

### Current Situation
Maria's restaurant is good. Her Yelp reviews are 4.5 stars. But she's noticed that new restaurants nearby are busier than hers, and she suspects it's because they show up higher on Google. She tried Googling "best Italian restaurant Portland" and her restaurant was on page 3. The restaurant on page 1 isn't even better than hers — she's eaten there.

### Pain Points
- "I don't know why I'm not showing up on Google."
- "I tried reading about SEO but it's all technical jargon."
- "I don't have time to learn marketing — I'm running a kitchen."
- "I looked at Semrush and it was $139/month. That's a lot for something I don't understand."
- "Someone told me I need to 'optimize my Google Business Profile' but I don't know what that means."

### What She Wants
- A tool that says: "Here's why you're not on page one. Here's exactly what to fix. It'll take 30 minutes."
- Something under $50/month
- "Tell me if it's working. Did my phone ring more this month?"
- "I don't want to become an SEO expert. I want to cook."

1. She Googles "why is my restaurant not on Google" → finds Goodly blog post
2. She runs the free audit → sees her score is 52/100
3. Revenue impact says: "You're losing an estimated $1,200/month in potential customers"
